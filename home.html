About MixWich

MIxWich is a rapidly growing Indonesian fast-food brand established in 2020 that offers premium, fully customizable sandwiches across its Jakarta, Surabaya, and Denpasar branches. Renowned for its commitment to freshness, affordability, and high-quality artisan ingredients, the brand is now expanding its digital footprint by developing a modern, clean, and responsive company profile website.
